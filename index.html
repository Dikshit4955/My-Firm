A Practice Built on Expertise & Trust

"We do not merely crunch numbers — we architect financial clarity and fight for every client's rightful position before the law."

Kapoor Dikshit and Associates is a proprietorship Chartered Accountancy firm established in 2017 and headquartered in Ludhiana, Punjab. The firm specialises in FEMA matters, GST & Income Tax litigation and appeals, CMA report drafting for bank finances, GST returns, income tax returns, and complete financial management services.

From first-generation entrepreneurs filing their first GST return to businesses fighting complex disputes before the GST Appellate Authority and Income Tax Appellate Tribunal (ITAT) — we bring the same meticulous dedication and personalised attention to every client.
